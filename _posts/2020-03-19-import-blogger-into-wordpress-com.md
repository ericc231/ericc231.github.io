![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d8811.04.30.png?w=1024)

WordPress有支援Blogger的文章、頁面備份，接著到Blogger去備份文章．進入「其他」的設定頁面（設定->其他)

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d888.56.37.png?w=445)

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d888.56.53.png?w=302)

點擊備份內容按鈕就會開始下載xml檔案

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d888.57.08.png?w=1024)

將檔案拿到WordPress.com匯入，結果會發生錯誤

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d8811.04.30-2.png?w=1024)

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d8811.04.38.png?w=1024)

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d8811.05.11.png?w=1024)

錯誤的原因是因為作者名字不同！如果發生這情形，得修改下載的xml檔案，把作者名字修改成WordPress.com上面的使用者名字，這樣子就可以成功匯入了．

![](https://ericchen231.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/e688aae59c96-2020-03-19-e4b88be58d8811.06.32.png?w=1024)
